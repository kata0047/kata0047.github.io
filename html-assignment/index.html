About me

Hello, my name is Yashswin Kataria, and I'm a student in Algonquin College's Interactive Media Design department. I am a native of Delhi, India's capital state. I am now in my home country, but I hope to be in Ottawa before the end of the semester. My interest in design began when I was a teenager. After high school, I wanted to pursue a profession in design. In my own country, I completed a one-year foundation diploma in Art and Design. Here in Canada, I opted for Interactive media design as my specialization to learn about graphic design, web development, and photography. My short-term goal is to enhance my design capabilities and prepare myself to live as an international student. My long-term goal, on the other hand, is to pursue my dream of being a graphic designer and climbing the success ladder to become a successful entrepreneur by establishing my own design studio.

In addition, my interests include trekking to new places, entertaining people, and cooking. I enjoy doing sports and going to the gym to stay in shape. During my school days, I ranked 2nd in an inter-school high jump tournament. In my spare time, I enjoy singing, watching videos of various performers, and watching football. I've always wanted to visit the USA since my favorite football team is the New Orleans Saints, which were founded in America. In particular, I'd like to visit NYC and see Times Square. The state's aesthetic architecture is visually pleasing and appealing.
